blogR

Pop data analysis and R for the web

{{ page.author }} (@benjaminlmoore)
Edinburgh PsychStats R-users, November 13th 2014


Slides online at: blm.io/talks/blogr

Point of this talk: try to convince you that with the R skills you have + statistics and good data visualisation, you have a powerful toolkit not just for research but also answering everyday questions that you're interested in. If you're interested chances are others will be too, you can share your results online and this could lead back to maybe an off-beat paper, or publicity, online articles and more.
